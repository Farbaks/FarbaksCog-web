TMT

Welcome to the Trail Making Test (TMT). This is a cognitive test of visual attention and task switching. This test can provide information about visual search speed, scanning, speed of processing, mental flexibility, and executive functioning. It comprises of two parts - TMT A and B.

TMT A

Welcome to the Trail Making Test (TMT) A. This test consists of 25 circles numbered 1 - 25. You are expected to connect the numbers by tapping on each circle in ascending order. This is a timed test and should be completed as quickly as possible.
